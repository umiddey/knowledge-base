Carbon Capture Solutions
EMPOWERING DIRECT AIR CAPTURE WITH EFFICIENT THERMAL SOLUTIONS
Direct Air Capture (DAC) is revolutionising how we address global carbon emissions. This groundbreaking technology captures CO₂ directly from ambient air, offering a scalable solution to remove greenhouse gases and reduce global temperatures. However, DAC systems are energy-intensive, requiring precise temperature control to optimise performance and minimise energy consumption. That’s where our expertise comes in.
Our industrial heat exchangers and capture towers are designed to meet the unique thermal challenges of DAC, providing efficient heat or mass transfer, temperature regulation, and energy recovery. Whether it’s cooling systems, air-liquid exchangers, or regenerative heat exchangers, we offer tailor-made solutions that enhance the efficiency and sustainability of DAC processes.
CARBON CAPTURE BY DESUBLIMATORS
Carbon capture by desublimation is an excellent alternative to other complicated separation methods. All you need is a cold source, so this is a great solution for LNG plants or vessels.​
The purpose of desublimators is material separation through phase change.
Desublimation is a cryogenic material separating process.
Desublimation can be an attractive alternative to adsorption and absorption separation methods.
