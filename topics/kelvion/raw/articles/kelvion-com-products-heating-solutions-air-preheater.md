Material and design variants of modern heat exchangers
Finned-tube heat exchangers can be made from numerous materials: steel, stainless steel,  aluminium and other special materials. Plain-tube heat exchangers are used mostly where a fluid present around the tubes is so polluted that finned tubes can no longer be used. Steel and stainless steel versions as well as a hairpin design are also possible.

The advantages of our air preheaters at a glance:
Aerodynamically favourable core tube with roughly 3 to 10 times less hydraulic friction as compared with a round tube with considerably lower pressure drop and better heat transfer rate
Good corrosion protection due to hot-dip galvanising in a dipped bath and at the same time an optimum metal joint between core tube and fin
High thermal transmittance resulting in small heat exchanger surface
