# Quality Management

Quality
Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental Policy
Quality Management
We are committed to Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental (QHSE) excellence.
Our standards
As a global manufacturer of industrial heat exchangers for various markets around the world, we take our corporate responsibility regarding sustainability and environmental protection very seriously. However, we don't just talk about sustainability, we practise it. Every day – worldwide – with our innovative technologies and products, our production processes and our corporate activities.
We, the Management Board of Kelvion, are committed to Quality, Health, Safety and Environmental excellence based on the following principles:
Quality starts at management level and affects all employees;
We understand and strive to exceed the expectations of our customers and stakeholders;
We create an accident- and incident-free workspace and prevent occupational illnesses;
We prevent environmental pollution and reduce energy consumption, waste and emissions;
We manage quality, safety, health, environmental and energy-consuming processes appropriately;
We continuously measure our performance to achieve our targets;
We continuously improve products, processes and energy consumption;
We continuously identify risks and implement sustainable corrective and preventive solutions;
We provide appropriate resources for our daily work to achieve our QHSE targets;
We continuously inform and consult our employees and their representatives and encourage them proactively to participate;
We prioritise the purchase of energy efficient products and services;
We comply with all legal requirements as well as all applicable national and international codes and standards;
We are open, transparent, cooperative and keep our commitments.
