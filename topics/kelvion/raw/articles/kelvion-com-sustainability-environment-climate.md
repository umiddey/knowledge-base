# Environmental & climate

Environment & Climate
Resource-efficient, sustainable and certified
Environmental & climate
To achieve the goal of resource conservation and resource efficiency, Kelvion uses the key instrument of environmental management. This involves planning, implementing, monitoring and improving processes to minimise the environmental impacts of an organisation.
Taking responsibility:
Our environmental strategy with ISO 14001 certification
Measures for climate protection and environmental protection within the company are developed strategically and established within an environmental strategy. We take environmental responsibility seriously: Certification according to ISO 14001 is recognised as a standard in environmental management and supports us and other companies in continuously improving our environmental performance.
The benefits of sustainable environmental management at Kelvion
Through our focus on sustainability, we protect natural resources and secure them for future generations.
With our environmental management, we reduce the company's ecological footprint and empower customers with our solutions to produce energy efficiently.
Thanks to sustainable practices, we can save costs in the long term, as these improve energy efficiency and reduce waste.
Sustainable environmental management at Kelvion
Our standards and certifications in environmental management
The implementation of environmental standards and certification according to ISO 14001 are essential steps for companies such as Kelvion that take their responsibility for environmental protection seriously. Through an effective environmental management system, we increase resource efficiency and conserve resources. At Kelvion, we can optimise your operations through targeted climate protection measures and so contribute to environmental protection.
Compliance with environmental regulations is also a key part of our sustainable environmental management. Environmental protection includes employee training and integrating environmentally friendly processes that increase our sustainability.
Hydrogen solutions for climate protection
With technical solutions for the hydrogen industry, we consider ourselves at the forefront in sustainable energy conversion. Hydrogen can be obtained from sources including fossil fuels, nuclear energy, biomass and renewable energies. There are different processes for producing hydrogen that determine the type of hydrogen generated. We focus on green hydrogen as it is produced in a climate-neutral way.
