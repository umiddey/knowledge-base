The heat exchangers can be equipped with plain tubes, low-finned tubes, or compact fin technology. Compact fin technology is especially effective for oil- and air-cooling, resulting in a powerful and compact heat exchanger that can save up to 75% in size compared to a conventional heat exchanger with plain tubes.
The heat exchangers of our Smart series are modular units built with standardized parts reaching a great balance between cost-effectiveness and configurability. For more demanding applications, the heat exchangers of our Advanced series can be customized to your requirements.
Additionally, if you need a tailor-made heat exchanger or insert bundle, to be integrated in your series product, our dedicated team is ready to work with you.
