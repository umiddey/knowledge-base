What is the significance of cooling processes in heavy and light industry?
In heavy and light industry, cooling processes influence how efficient, safe and durable the production processes and manufactured products are. For this reason, modern cooling systems are crucial. In addition to process quality and optimised performance, there are other requirements to be met, such as avoiding overheating and complying with environmental standards. In industries where materials and machinery are exposed to extreme temperatures, precise cooling processes help to maintain product quality. By reusing the thermal energy generated in the production process, the use of industrial chillers also enables significant energy savings and lower operating costs.
