# Dielectric modular air cooler

Transformer Cooling Systems
Dielectric Modular Air Cooler
Complete turnkey solutions for liquid cooling
Dielectric modular air cooler
Kelvion Dielectric Modular Air Coolers are immediately ready for use and can easily be connected to an immersion cooling system. This solution consists of three core elements: A high performance aluminium cooler, an axial fan and a durable and maintenance friendly Dielectric Pump. The compact and lightweight design makes it easy to transport and connections can be adapted according to customer specifications. Kelvion Dielectric Mobile Air Coolers suit perfectly for Containerised systems with immersion cooling.
