Mark Bevan
Chief Financial Officer
Mark Bevan was appointed Chief Financial Officer (CFO) in September 2022. He is a results-driven finance professional with an impressive track record in accounting, business improvement and strategy development across a broad range of industrial sectors.
Mark brings nearly 30 years of leadership experience from a wide range of finance and business functions including various senior financial and CFO positions at companies such as Mayborn Group, JCB Excavators Ltd., and the Staffline Group.

Andy Blandford
Chief Executive Officer
Andy Blandford joined Kelvion as Chief Executive Officer (CEO) in January 2022. Andy is a highly experienced executive with extensive expertise in delivering significant growth and strong results, and building high-performance teams.
Andy previously worked at Dematic, a leading provider of supply chain solutions, where he served as SVP Execution/Operations for EMEA and before that as the SVP/Managing Director for Northern Europe. His earlier experience includes General Management roles at CNH Industrial, AGCO Corporation and JCB Excavators Ltd.
Andy has deep international leadership experience having lived and worked in several countries in Europe, the Americas and APAC.

Najat Bouamoud
Enterprise Transformation
Najat Bouamoud joined Kelvion in September 2018 and heads the Enterprise Transformation function.
Before joining Kelvion, Najat held various leadership roles at Accenture where she worked in IT Strategy and Transformation.
Najat has over 10 years of experience in project management and leadership. She has been instrumental in leading complex projects, accelerating business processes and achieving organisational excellence in multiple industries.

Michael Bruneau
Region Americas
Michael Bruneau joined Kelvion in January 2024 and is leading our Americas region. He is a results-driven business leader with over 25 years’ experience derived from multiple industries and business functions. He has a proven track record in business growth, leading turnarounds and enabling international teams to new heights of performance.
Michael developed his career at Danaher/Fortive, before moving to other businesses driving transformation and operational excellence. He has also held senior leadership roles at Wabtec, BAE Systems and Red Dot Corporation.

Harald Gössl
Technology
Harald Gössl joined Kelvion in 2019 and is now responsible for Technology, following 30 years in global operational roles where he consistently delivered results as a turnaround manager. He has a sound knowledge of processes and procedures in a project-based, low volume business and is a strong believer in lean methods to improve workplace safety and efficiency.
Harald has previously held executive positions in China, Germany and Austria with a strong focus on lean production, supply chain, product lifecycle management and digital processes.

Philipp Kurney
Region EMEA

Miguel Marcos

Uli Stadermann
Legal

Steffen Thierfelder
Commercial
Steffen Thierfelder joined Kelvion in July 2022 and is responsible for the commercial organisation. He has a proven track record of significant revenue growth and improvement in profitability and has international experience in General Management, Service and Sales roles.
Steffen was previously the Managing Director for the UK and Ireland at a leading supplier of automated systems for distribution centres, and also held senior leadership roles at both Prettl Group and CNH Industrial.

Maarten Verkiel
Manufacturing
Maarten Verkiel joined Kelvion in November 2022 and took over responsibility for Manufacturing in 2024. He has broad experience in international leadership, general management of technical customer-focused businesses and a solid background in engineering and manufacturing.
Maarten held various international leadership roles at Caterpillar and has lived and worked in China, the UK and the USA. His career highlights include measurable safety improvements, leading his team to win the global TKE manufacturing quality award in 2021, successfully introducing new products in the market and implementing operational excellence.

Jonathan Yuk
Region APAC
Jonathan Yuk joined Kelvion in September 2025 and leads our APAC region. Jonathan is an accomplished international executive with over 20 years of global leadership experience. Throughout his career, Jonathan has demonstrated a strong track record of driving sustainable growth, fostering innovation, and developing high-performing teams.

Hubertus M. Mühlhäuser
Chairman
Hubertus has over 20 years of experience in senior positions at various public and private companies within the global industrials sector.
He has served as Chairman & CEO of Pontem Corporation, a special purpose acquisition company listed on the NYSE. He was also CEO & Executive Director of CNH Industrial (NYSE:CNHI), one of the largest capital goods companies, overseeing global brands such as Iveco, Magirus, Steyr, New Holland and Case, until 2020.
Hubertus has also held the role of CEO & Executive Director at Welbilt Inc. (NYSE:WBT), a leading food-service manufacturer, where he successfully led its NYSE Spin-Off. Prior to this, he was Senior Vice President & General Manager at AGCO Corp. (NYSE:AGCO), a major agricultural OEM with brands including Fendt, Valtra and Massey Ferguson.
