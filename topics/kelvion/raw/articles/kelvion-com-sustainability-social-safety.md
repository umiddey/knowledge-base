# Safety and social responsibility

Social & Safety
Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI)
Safety and social responsibility
The health of our employees is one of our top priorities. We aim to provide them with a safe working environment and ensure their wellbeing. Our vision is to continue pursuing our zero-accident strategy to reduce the number of accidents and ensure safe conditions and actions.
Embracing diversity
A strong foundation of our corporate culture
Kelvion is committed to providing appropriate workplaces, implementing improvement suggestions, and continuously monitoring their effectiveness. Kelvion’s ESG strategy aims to create a responsible corporate culture and an inspiring work environment. Our employees’ diversity and inclusion at all levels of the company contribute significantly to this.
Our employees are our most valuable asset, and we treat everyone equally. Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Management is an integral part of our personnel policy, and we reject any form of discrimination. Appropriate programmes to ensure this stance worldwide, such as the DEI policy, are in place.
What we stand for:
Kelvion takes corporate responsibility for the integration of socially responsible working conditions. We are committed to the principles of social responsibility towards our employees and society, offering fair working conditions worldwide.
We reject any form of discrimination – whether based on gender, sexual identity, origin, skin colour or other personal characteristics. We see ourselves as a socially responsible employer who treats our employees with respect.
Inclusion, equality and integration play a key role at Kelvion. Our goal is to have a diverse and talented team that also reflects the diversity of our stakeholders and business partners.
Kelvion has introduced the Golden Safety Rules to further improve safety as a priority within the company and support the zero-accidents target. As part of this initiative, we have employee training with detailed materials and instructions developed for our production and service staff.
Kelvion’s ‘Safety Topic of the Month’ (STM) is an initiative for our employees and published monthly to raise awareness of a specific safety topic.
We have also defined optimised Health, Safety and Environmental Management (HSE) at all Kelvion production sites. This includes questionnaires for employees for self-assessment and self-evaluation, which are divided into categories such as goals, qualifications and risk assessment.
