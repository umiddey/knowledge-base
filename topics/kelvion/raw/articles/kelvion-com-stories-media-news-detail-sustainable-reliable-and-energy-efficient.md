# Sustainable, reliable and energy efficient

Sustainable, reliable and energy efficient
KELVION LAUNCHES NEW HIGH PRESSURE LASER-WELDED CASSETTE FOR INDUSTRIAL HEAT PUMPS
Sustainable, reliable and energy efficient
Solutions
08.02.2024
The new Kelvion HPL100S (high pressure laser-welded cassette) offers a sustainable, energy efficient and reliable heat exchange solution to the industrial heat pump market. “Supporting customers with reliable and energy efficient heat exchange solutions is Kelvion’s priority”, says Michael Henningsen, Product Line Manager GPHE (Gasketed Plate Heat Exchanger). “We are strongly positioned in green and high-tech, and we are convinced that our energy efficient heat exchange solutions provide the best support for companies facing global climate challenges.”
Industrial heat pumps have a key role to play in achieving global CO2 emission reduction targets, including recovering waste heat for district heating and industrial processes. With the HPL series, Kelvion has combined the advantages of a highly efficient plate and a laser-welded cassette. The innovative semi-welded plate heat exchanger is suitable for commercial and industrial heat pump solutions and provides customers with unique technology for applications with extremely high pressures up to 63 bar-g and temperatures up to 170 °C.
