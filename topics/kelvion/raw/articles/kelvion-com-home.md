Hydrogen
In the world of renewable energy, green hydrogen plays a key role, especially in Germany, where the production and manufacture of green hydrogen is being driven forward using advanced technologies. By using electrolysis processes that utilize renewable energy sources, water is split into hydrogen and oxygen. Leading technologies in this field include alkaline electrolysis (AEC) and proton exchange membrane electrolysis (PEMEC), both of which are known for their efficiency in the environmentally friendly production of hydrogen. Kelvion also contributes to sustainable energy production with its innovative heat exchange solutions.
