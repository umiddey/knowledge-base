# Adiabiatic Systems

Heat Rejection & Heat Recovery Solutions
Adiabiatic Systems
Innovative cooling systems with low operating costs
Adiabiatic Systems
In an adiabatic system, the evaporation energy of the water provides cooling of the air. An adiabatic system results in cooling of the air at the dry cooler. Cooling of 10 °C can be achieved. Our solutions can be equipped with spray nozzles or pads and depending on the conditions, can be perfectly adapted to your application. They offer safety at high ambient temperatures, optimised water use and improved performance.
