# NVIDIA Blackwell Architecture

NVIDIA's Blackwell generation is the successor to Hopper and the current flagship AI GPU architecture family.

## What it covers
- Multi-die package design and high-bandwidth interconnects
- Fifth-generation Tensor Cores and new low-precision support
- Transformer Engine improvements for large-model training and inference
- Memory bandwidth and capacity increases for frontier model workloads
- Data center packaging and system-level deployment considerations
