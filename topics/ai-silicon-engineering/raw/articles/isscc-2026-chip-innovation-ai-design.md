# ISSCC 2026: Chip Innovation, AI Enabled Design And Training

## MediaTek CEO Rick Tsai on AI's Impact
- Cloud data center spending expected to increase 87% between 2025-2030
- Semiconductor market 53% higher by 2029 (16% CAGR vs 9% without AI buildout)
- Before 2030, data centers could consume all world's energy production at current trajectory — non-sustainable

### Key Metrics for Sustainability
- Performance per total cost of ownership (Perf/TCO)
- Performance per watt (Perf/Watt)
- Co-optimization across compute, scale-up and scale-out interconnects
- Heterogeneous computing, new packaging approaches

### Memory Developments
Various options for system memory in AI applications: high-bandwidth flash, DDR, and HBM memory.

## Cadence CEO Anirudh Devgan on AI in Design
- 4.5x growth in AI inference power consumption between 2025-2030
- AI can improve design performance up to 100x
- Cadence creating own LLMs trained on chip design data
- Cadence "super agent" for digital design

## Apple's Hope Giles on Next-Gen Engineers
- Number of EEs in US schools had been declining while IC complexity increases
- Trend may be reversing based on 2023 data
- Close collaboration between Apple and universities increasing enrollment in VLSI classes
