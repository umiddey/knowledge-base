# TSMC's CoWoS Monopoly

## Key Thesis
TSMC achieved what research labs couldn't: industrializing 2.5D packaging with yield, scale, and strategic collaboration. While NVIDIA gets the spotlight, CoWoS packaging is the unsung hero.

## ASP (Average Selling Price) Drivers
- **Base Die ASP**: 3nm is ~15-20% higher than 5nm
- **Packaged ASP (CoWoS + HBM)**: Adds ~25-35% premium over base die
- **Final Module ASP**: Additional system-level integration further increases margins
- 3nm/5nm processes contributed 74% of wafer revenue in Q2 2025

## Competitive Landscape
- Intel's EMIB (bridged 2.5D) and Foveros (3D logic stacking) haven't matched CoWoS in scale or commercial traction
- CoWoS enables tightly coupled GPU + HBM stacks on silicon interposer: lower latency, higher bandwidth, compact and power-efficient
- CoWoS production capacity has been a bottleneck for AI chip output
