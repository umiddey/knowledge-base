# 2026 Global Semiconductor Industry Outlook (Deloitte)

Gen AI chips are likely to account for about 50% of industry revenues in 2026, but they represent less than 20 million chips out of the trillion+ chips manufactured annually. The vast majority of chips remain non-AI — IoT, automotive, industrial, consumer electronics.

Key tension: massive revenue concentration in a tiny fraction of chip volume creates supply chain and market concentration risks.
