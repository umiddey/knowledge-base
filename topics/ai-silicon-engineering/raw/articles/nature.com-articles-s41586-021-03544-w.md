# Chip design with reinforcement learning

Google's AlphaChip line of work applies reinforcement learning to macro placement and floorplanning.

## Core ideas
- Represent chip floorplanning as a sequential decision problem
- Optimize objectives such as wirelength, congestion, and density
- Use learned policies to produce chip layouts faster than manual flows
- Apply the method to large accelerator designs

## Why it matters
- This is the canonical AI-for-chip-design source for RL-based floorplanning
- It connects directly to physical design in the VLSI flow
- It is one of the clearest examples of ML changing silicon engineering practice
