# In-Datacenter Performance Analysis of a Tensor Processing Unit

This is one of the canonical Google TPU papers and a core source for understanding TPU system behavior in production settings.

## Core ideas
- Datacenter-level evaluation rather than isolated microbenchmarks
- Comparison of TPU workloads against real serving and training demands
- System bottlenecks, throughput characteristics, and efficiency tradeoffs
- A hardware-software co-design perspective on ML acceleration

## Why it matters
- It explains why TPUs are more than just a matrix-multiply unit
- It grounds the TPU discussion in real workload analysis
- It is foundational for the `google-tpu-architecture` concept
