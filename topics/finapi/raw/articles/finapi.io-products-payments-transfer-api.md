# SEPA Bank Transfers via finAPI Payment API

## What finAPI Payments Covers

- Standard SEPA bank transfers
- Scheduled transfers (deferred execution date)
- Standing orders (recurring)
- Instant Payments (SEPA Instant Credit Transfer)
- Batch/collective transfers (multiple payments in one step)
- Payment links / QR codes

## Key Benefits

- BaFin-licensed Payment Initiation Service (PIS)
- BaFin-registered Account Information Service (AIS)
- Low fees
- Integrates into accounting software, e-commerce checkouts
- Supports both business-to-business and consumer payment flows

## Use Case Context

finAPI positions payments as part of a broader open banking platform. The payments product is used for:
- Accounting software automation (pay invoices via API)
- E-commerce checkout (replace manual IBAN entry)
- Instant payment use cases (delivery on receipt, license activation)
- Salary/invoice batch runs via collective transfers

## Product Suite (Payments)

- Bank transfer (SEPA Credit Transfer)
- Direct debit
- Instant Payments
- Payment & Identity check (combined KYC + payment)
- Shopware Payments Plugin

## Regulatory Context

finAPI holds a BaFin PIS license, meaning it is a regulated Third Party Provider (TPP) under PSD2. This is what allows it to initiate payments from user bank accounts without the business needing their own banking license.
