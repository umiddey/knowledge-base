# finAPI Platform Overview

## Company Facts

- Founded 2008 (Munich, Germany)
- BaFin-licensed Payment Initiation Service Provider (PIS)
- BaFin-registered Account Information Service Provider (AIS)
- TÜV-certified for data privacy (Trusted Site Privacy)
- 100M+ potential bank accounts connected
- 20M+ daily API calls
- €7 billion+ monthly payment volume
- 13 European markets covered
- Servers located in Germany (data residency)

## Product Portfolio

### Open Banking API
- Account data access (AIS)
- Payment initiation (PIS)
- PSD2-compliant XS2A server for banks
- Customers can use finAPI's PSD2 license (don't need their own)

### Data Intelligence
- Transaction categorization
- Cash flow analysis
- Contract detection
- Credit risk (KreditCheck / LoanValidator)
- Digital account check (for lending/onboarding)

### Payments
- SEPA Credit Transfer
- SEPA Direct Debit
- SEPA Instant Credit Transfer
- Standing orders
- Batch/collective transfers
- Payment links / QR codes

### KYC / GiroIdent
- Identity verification via bank account analysis + SCHUFA data
- AMLA-compliant customer identification
- Age verification, address validation

## What Makes finAPI Distinctive

1. **No PSD2 license required** — customers use finAPI's BaFin license via Web Form 2.0
2. **German market depth** — deep bank connectivity in DACH region
3. **Data intelligence** — goes beyond pure payment initiation into financial analytics
4. **Compliance-first** — German regulatory environment makes compliance a feature, not an afterthought
5. **Free 30-day trial** — standard for developer onboarding

## Positioning

finAPI is positioned as a full open banking platform, not just a payment API. Its data intelligence products differentiate it from pure-play payment providers. In Germany specifically, it has among the deepest bank coverage.
