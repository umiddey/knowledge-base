# Alluzience

Alluzience is Galderma’s ready-to-use liquid botulinum toxin type A product for the European market. Galderma positioned it as the first ready-to-use liquid neuromodulator in Europe, aimed at temporary improvement of moderate to severe glabellar lines. The point of the product is not only efficacy, but convenience: no reconstitution, simpler workflow, and a premium workflow-friendly proposition for clinics.

Alluzience matters because it shows the company’s toxin strategy is not limited to older legacy brands. Galderma uses Alluzience to modernize the neuromodulator category and to build a bridge between Dysport and newer products like Relfydess. In a competitive aesthetics market, workflow and ease-of-use can matter as much as pure pharmacology.

The product is also useful as a licensing example. Galderma has an exclusive license from Ipsen to develop, promote, and distribute Alluzience in Europe. That means the brand is part of Galderma’s growth engine even though it is not a wholly original molecule.

## Key Points
- Alluzience is a ready-to-use liquid neuromodulator.
- It was positioned as the first such product in Europe.
- The product targets glabellar lines.
- The brand shows Galderma’s mix of proprietary development and licensed commercialization.

## Example
A busy aesthetic clinic can use Alluzience to reduce preparation time for toxin injections because the product is ready to inject. That convenience can improve throughput and makes the brand attractive in a high-volume practice.
