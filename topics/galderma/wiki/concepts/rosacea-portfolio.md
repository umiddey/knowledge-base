# Rosacea Portfolio

Galderma’s rosacea portfolio shows how the company builds around a single disease area with multiple product types. The lineup includes Soolantra, Oracea, Mirvaso, and EPSOLAY. Together they cover inflammatory lesions, oral treatment, redness, and bumps and blemishes. That diversity matters because rosacea is not one symptom but a cluster of symptoms that may require different mechanisms of action.

This is a good example of franchise thinking inside Therapeutic Dermatology. Instead of relying on a single brand, Galderma can address different patient presentations and treatment preferences. That makes the rosacea business more resilient and gives physicians more reason to stay inside the same company’s ecosystem.

The portfolio also shows how prescription dermatology supports recurring use. Rosacea is chronic, often needs maintenance, and can be approached with combinations of therapies. That makes the category a reliable commercial lane even if it lacks the headline growth of Nemluvio.

## Key Points
- Rosacea is served by multiple Galderma brands, not one product.
- Soolantra and EPSOLAY treat inflammatory lesions and bumps.
- Oracea supports oral treatment.
- Mirvaso addresses facial redness.

## Example
A patient with papulopustular rosacea might use Soolantra for inflammatory lesions, Oracea for oral support, and Mirvaso for redness. Galderma can cover the disease from several angles.
