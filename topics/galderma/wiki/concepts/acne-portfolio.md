# Acne Portfolio

Galderma’s acne portfolio spans consumer, over-the-counter, and prescription treatments. That matters because acne is a disease area with multiple severity levels and multiple buying behaviors. The portfolio includes Differin, AKLIEF, TWYNEO, and Benzac, each serving a different part of the acne journey. Differin is the consumer-facing retinoid story, AKLIEF is a newer prescription retinoid, TWYNEO is a licensed fixed-dose combination, and Benzac is a consumer regimen brand.

The portfolio is valuable because it lets Galderma meet patients at different stages of care. A patient can begin with OTC acne treatment, move into prescription therapy if needed, and still remain inside Galderma’s ecosystem. The company’s sources show that this is not accidental; it is a deliberate way to build recurring category relevance rather than a single-product acne business.

This also shows why Galderma is strong in dermatology as a commercial discipline. Acne is common, visible, and recurring, so brands that combine clinical credibility with broad access can keep generating demand across life stages.

## Key Points
- The portfolio spans OTC and prescription treatments.
- Differin gives Galderma consumer acne access.
- AKLIEF adds newer retinoid chemistry.
- TWYNEO adds a licensed combination product.
- Benzac supports regimen-based consumer use.

## Example
A teenager may start with Benzac or Differin, then later move to a prescription like TWYNEO or AKLIEF if acne remains uncontrolled. Galderma can capture that whole sequence rather than only one product step.
