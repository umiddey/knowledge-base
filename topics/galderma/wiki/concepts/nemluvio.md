# Nemluvio

Nemluvio is Galderma’s biologic growth asset and the centerpiece of Therapeutic Dermatology. The active ingredient is nemolizumab, an interleukin-31 receptor antagonist. The FDA approved it in August 2024 for prurigo nodularis, and the EMA later expanded its European use to atopic dermatitis and prurigo nodularis. By 2025, Galderma was describing Nemluvio as a potential blockbuster and a major driver of segment growth.

The reason Nemluvio matters commercially is simple: itch-driven inflammatory skin disease is a large unmet-need area, and the drug has a clear clinical story. Galderma reported $39 million in Q1 2025 sales, $131 million in H1 2025 sales, and stronger growth through the rest of the year. In the full-year 2025 results, Nemluvio was central to the Therapeutic Dermatology segment’s 50.2% constant-currency growth.

Nemluvio is also important strategically because it changes Galderma’s mix. It moves the company further into high-value prescription biologics and reduces dependence on aesthetics alone. That makes it the most important growth asset outside the injectable business.

The latest annual report sharpened the launch story. Galderma said Nemluvio reached 452 million USD in 2025 sales and was now approved in the EU, the U.K., Switzerland, and Singapore for atopic dermatitis and prurigo nodularis, with further launches in Canada and South Korea and more submissions underway. The report also noted that new-patient starts in the U.S. were running at about 35% market share in prurigo nodularis and about 8% in atopic dermatitis in early 2026, which is unusually concrete evidence of traction for a biologic launch.

## Key Points
- Nemluvio is nemolizumab, an IL-31 receptor antagonist.
- FDA approval came in August 2024 for prurigo nodularis.
- EMA approvals extended its European label to atopic dermatitis and prurigo nodularis.
- The drug drove a large share of Therapeutic Dermatology growth in 2025.
- By 2026 the launch had moved from approval story to market-share story.

## Example
A patient with severe prurigo nodularis and relentless itch may receive Nemluvio after topical options fail. That treatment is very different from a cosmetic filler, but it is equally important to Galderma’s revenue mix.
