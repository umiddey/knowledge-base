# Relfydess

Relfydess is Galderma’s newest liquid neuromodulator and one of the clearest examples of the company’s effort to modernize its aesthetics portfolio. Galderma describes it as the first and only ready-to-use liquid neuromodulator created with PEARL Technology. The appeal is simple: no reconstitution, strong activity, and convenient clinic handling. The product was developed and manufactured by Galderma specifically to expand its neuromodulator franchise.

Relfydess matters because it is not just another toxin. It sits at the intersection of science, workflow, and premium positioning. Galderma uses clinical-trial language, rapid onset claims, and duration data to show that this product is meant to compete on performance and convenience at the same time. That makes it important in a segment where clinic efficiency matters.

The brand is also a signal about Galderma’s innovation engine. Rather than relying on a single legacy toxin, the company is trying to build a ladder of neuromodulator offerings that can serve different clinics and patient preferences.

The IMCAS 2025 material adds the useful clinic-level details. Galderma says Relfydess is optimized for simple volumetric dosing, without reconstitution, and that it is designed to preserve molecule integrity. That matters because injectors care about time, consistency, and predictability, not only about headline efficacy. The company’s own data points also suggest fast onset and durable maintenance, which are the exact attributes you want in a premium neuromodulator.

## Key Points
- Relfydess is a ready-to-use liquid neuromodulator.
- It was developed and manufactured by Galderma.
- PEARL Technology is part of the differentiation story.
- The product is meant to expand Galderma’s neuromodulator portfolio.
- Galderma emphasizes clinic efficiency, dose consistency, and durability as much as clinical effect.

## Example
A high-volume injector wants a toxin product that avoids reconstitution and can be dosed consistently. Relfydess is built for that use case, which is why workflow is part of the brand story.
