# Regenerative Aesthetics Stack

Galderma’s strongest aesthetic opportunity is not a single product but a stack: Restylane for structure, Sculptra for collagen-driven volume restoration, ALASTIN for peri-procedural support, and Relfydess for convenient neuromodulation. That stack matters most when a patient is dealing with facial aging from both normal aging and rapid weight loss.

## Example
A patient who lost weight on a GLP-1 drug may need a chin profile correction, midface support, and better skin quality. A Galderma-aligned plan could use Restylane Lyft for structure, Sculptra for longer-term volume support, ALASTIN for skin recovery, and Relfydess for wrinkle management.

## Analysis
This connection matters because it explains why Galderma keeps talking about “regenerative” aesthetics. The company is not merely stacking unrelated products. It is building a workflow that spans immediate correction, longer-durability volume restoration, and peri-procedural skin care. That is a more complete commercial offer than a filler-only or toxin-only business.
