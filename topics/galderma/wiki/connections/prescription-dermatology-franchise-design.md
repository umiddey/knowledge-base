# Prescription Dermatology Franchise Design

Galderma’s prescription dermatology business is organized around disease areas rather than individual products. Acne, rosacea, and pigmentary disorders each have multiple brands that serve different symptom profiles or treatment steps. Nemluvio adds a biologic growth layer on top of that franchise structure. This is why the segment is more durable than a simple “single-drug” story.

## Example
A patient might start on Differin, move to AKLIEF or TWYNEO if acne persists, use Soolantra or EPSOLAY for rosacea symptoms, and require Tri-Luma for melasma. Another patient with a completely different disease might go straight to Nemluvio.

## Analysis
The portfolio design matters because it reduces dependence on any one product while still concentrating commercial effort in dermatology. Galderma can keep physicians within one prescription system across multiple disease states. The downside is that each disease area needs its own regulatory and clinical evidence, but the upside is a broader, more defensible franchise model.
