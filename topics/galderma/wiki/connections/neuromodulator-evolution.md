# Neuromodulator Evolution

Galderma’s neuromodulator story has evolved from legacy Dysport to ready-to-use products like Alluzience and Relfydess. The key shift is not just chemistry but workflow. The newer products are designed to reduce preparation friction and improve convenience for injectors, which is a meaningful commercial advantage in high-volume aesthetic practices.

## Example
A clinic might have originally used Dysport as its toxin workhorse. If the same clinic wants a ready-to-inject option with less prep, it could move to Alluzience or Relfydess depending on geography and product availability.

## Analysis
This is a classic innovation path: keep the core franchise but create a premium convenience layer above it. Galderma does not need to abandon Dysport to grow; it can use newer toxins to widen the portfolio and serve different clinic preferences. That creates a product ladder rather than a single-line dependency.
