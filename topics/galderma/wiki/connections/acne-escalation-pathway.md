# Acne Escalation Pathway

Galderma’s acne business is structured around escalation. Differin gives the company the consumer/OTC entry point, while AKLIEF and TWYNEO provide prescription options for patients who need more intensive treatment. Benzac supports regimen-style consumer use, which means the company can participate in acne care across different severity levels and access channels.

## Example
A patient might begin with Differin or Benzac for mild acne, then step up to AKLIEF or TWYNEO when OTC treatment is not enough. That is a straightforward clinical ladder, but it is also a commercial retention strategy.

## Analysis
The escalation pathway matters because acne is one of the easiest dermatology areas to understand as a funnel. Galderma can capture patients early, then move them toward more advanced and more profitable prescription products as needed. That makes Differin strategically important even though it is not the highest-revenue acne brand.
