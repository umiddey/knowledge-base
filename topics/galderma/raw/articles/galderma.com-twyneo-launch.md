# Source Notes
- Galderma launched TWYNEO in the U.S. in 2022.
- The product is the first FDA-approved fixed-dose combination of tretinoin and benzoyl peroxide.
- The release emphasizes microencapsulation technology and rapid results.
- It was licensed from Sol-Gel for U.S. commercialization.
- TWYNEO is part of the acne-focused prescription dermatology portfolio.
- The launch also shows how Galderma fills portfolio gaps with licensed, differentiated topical assets.
