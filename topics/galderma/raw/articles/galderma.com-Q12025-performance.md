# Source Notes
- First quarter 2025 net sales were 1.129 billion USD, up 8.3% at constant currency.
- Galderma says the result was primarily driven by volume and favorable mix.
- Injectable Aesthetics grew 9.9%, Dermatological Skincare 7.8%, and Therapeutic Dermatology 4.9%.
- The release specifically calls out strong performance from two launches with blockbuster potential.
- Nemluvio sales were 39 million USD in the quarter.
- The company says the result benefited from favorable phasing in Injectable Aesthetics and a stronger-than-expected ramp-up of Nemluvio.
- This source is the early 2025 commercial baseline.
- It is useful for tracing the launch curve of Nemluvio and Relfydess.

# Why it matters
- Gives the first 2025 checkpoint after the 2024 base year.
- Useful for showing how Galderma started the year before the faster H2 ramp.
