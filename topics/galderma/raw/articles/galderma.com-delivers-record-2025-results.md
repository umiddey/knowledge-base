# Source Notes
- Full-year 2025 net sales were 5.207 billion USD, up 17.7% at constant currency.
- Galderma says growth was driven primarily by volume, with favorable mix helping offset pricing pressure.
- Segment growth in 2025 was strong across the board: Injectable Aesthetics up 11.5%, Dermatological Skincare up 9.3%, and Therapeutic Dermatology up 50.2%.
- Thermapeutic Dermatology growth was led by Nemluvio, which the company says has blockbuster potential.
- Within Injectable Aesthetics, neuromodulators and fillers/biostimulators both grew.
- The company raised its peak sales guidance for Nemluvio from above 2 billion USD to above 4 billion USD.
- Core EBITDA reached 1.211 billion USD, and leverage fell to 1.5x.
- The company says the U.S. and international markets both posted double-digit growth.
