# Source Notes
- Galderma launched EPSOLAY cream in the U.S. in 2022 after FDA approval.
- The product treats bumps and blemishes of rosacea and uses microencapsulated benzoyl peroxide.
- The release emphasizes tolerability and sustained relief.
- EPSOLAY was licensed from Sol-Gel for U.S. commercialization.
- It is a useful rosacea franchise addition alongside Mirvaso and Oracea.
- This product reinforces Galderma's strategy of building a deep disease-area franchise rather than a single-brand position.

# Why it matters
- Important source for the rosacea therapy revenue base.
- Shows Galderma’s commercial strategy in prescription dermatology.
- It expands the rosacea franchise beyond the older brands in the portfolio.
