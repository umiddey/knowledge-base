# Source Notes
- Dysport is Galderma’s botulinum toxin type A brand used in aesthetic and therapeutic indications.
- The page says it is indicated for temporary improvement of moderate to severe glabellar lines.
- It notes that Dysport is marketed as Azzalure in the EU for aesthetic use.
- Dysport is one of the core neuromodulators in Galderma’s Injectable Aesthetics franchise.
- This is the canonical product page for one of the company’s biggest aesthetic brands.
- It is also the historical anchor for Galderma's toxin portfolio before newer launches like Relfydess and Alluzience.

# Why it matters
- Essential source for understanding the neuromodulator revenue base.
- Important because Dysport remains a legacy but still central brand.
- It is the baseline against which newer neuromodulators have to prove themselves.
