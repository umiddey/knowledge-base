# Source Notes
- 2024 was a defining year because Galderma completed its IPO on the SIX Swiss Exchange.
- The report describes Galderma as the pure-play dermatology category leader.
- It says the company expanded across all segments and reached major milestones in growth, innovation, and education.
- The report highlights the launch of Nemluvio in the U.S. and the European approval of Relfydess.
- It states Cetaphil exceeded 1 billion USD in annual sales in 2023 and Alastin continued to expand.
- It emphasizes the integrated dermatology strategy and the breadth of the flagship portfolio.
- It provides a cleaner baseline for pre-2025 operating performance.
- It is the best bridge between the 2024 IPO era and the 2025 acceleration.
