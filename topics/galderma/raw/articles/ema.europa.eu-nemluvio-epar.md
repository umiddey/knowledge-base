# Source Notes
- The EMA says Nemluvio is used for adults and adolescents 12 years and older with atopic dermatitis and for adults with moderate-to-severe prurigo nodularis.
- The active substance is nemolizumab.
- The medicine is given by injection under the skin and requires prescription supervision.
