# Source Notes
- The FDA snapshot says NEMLUVIO is an interleukin-31 receptor antagonist.
- It is indicated for adults with prurigo nodularis.
- The approval date was August 12, 2024.
- The snapshot explains that the clinical program included two trials, OLYMPIA 1 and OLYMPIA 2, with 560 patients.
- It describes the dosing regimen and efficacy endpoints, including itch reduction and clear/almost-clear skin outcomes.
- The trial populations and subgroup analysis are documented in detail.
- The page is a useful regulatory source for the scientific basis of the drug.
