# Source Notes
- First-half 2025 net sales reached 2.448 billion USD, up 12.2% at constant currency.
- Growth was mainly volume-driven and broad-based across geographies.
- Injectable Aesthetics grew 9.8%, Dermatological Skincare grew 7.7%, and Therapeutic Dermatology grew 26.9%.
- Nemluvio delivered 131 million USD in sales in the first half of 2025.
- Relfydess was already launched in 17 markets by this point.
- The company says it gained share in Injectable Aesthetics in both geographies and in Dermatological Skincare in international markets.
- Core EBITDA was 555 million USD, with leverage at 2.1x.
- This source shows the early ramp of Nemluvio and Relfydess before full-year results.

# Why it matters
- Useful checkpoint between the 2024 base and the 2025 year-end outcome.
- Good evidence that Nemluvio was beginning to matter commercially in 2025.
- Helps separate one-off launch momentum from full-year contribution.
