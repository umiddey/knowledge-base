# Source Notes
- First nine months of 2025 net sales reached 3.737 billion USD, up 15.0% at constant currency.
- The company says growth was predominantly volume-led and accelerated in Q3.
- Injectable Aesthetics grew 10.5%, Dermatological Skincare 8.2%, and Therapeutic Dermatology 40.4%.
- Galderma says it gained share across both sub-segments of Injectable Aesthetics in both geographies.
- International markets grew 13.2% and the U.S. grew 17.5% at constant currency.
- The release says commercial execution and innovation momentum were strong across the portfolio.
- This is a useful source for the in-year inflection in 2025.
- It sits between the H1 release and full-year earnings and helps show trend continuity.

# Why it matters
- Confirms that the revenue story was not a one-quarter spike.
- Good support for understanding the cadence of 2025 growth.
