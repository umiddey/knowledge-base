# Source Notes
- Galderma describes Therapeutic Dermatology as prescription treatments for moderate-to-severe skin conditions from acne to skin cancer.
- The company says it holds leading positions in acne, rosacea, and non-melanoma skin cancer treatments.
- The page explicitly calls out Nemluvio as a key priority because it addresses unmet need in prurigo nodularis and atopic dermatitis.
- It also notes the FDA approvals of Nemluvio in 2024.
- The page says R&D is focused on biotechnologies for injectable and oral products.
- It presents therapeutic dermatology as a growth engine alongside aesthetics and skincare.

# Why it matters
- This is the best source for the prescription side of the portfolio.
- Useful for understanding how Nemluvio fits into the broader business rather than as a standalone launch.
