# Source Notes
- Galderma got FDA approval to move Differin Gel 0.1% from prescription to OTC use.
- The release says it was the first new FDA-approved active ingredient in OTC acne in over 30 years.
- Differin is presented as a prescription-strength retinoid with broad consumer access potential.
- This source is important for understanding Differin’s consumer-acne role.
- It also shows how Galderma uses regulatory milestones to expand access and volume.
- The OTC transition is a classic example of category expansion inside dermatology.

# Why it matters
- Strong source for the consumer acne channel and OTC strategy.
- Important because Differin is a major legacy acne brand.
- It is a clear example of moving prescription dermatology into mass consumer access.
