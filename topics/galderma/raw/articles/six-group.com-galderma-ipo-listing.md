# Source Notes
- SIX says Galderma began trading on March 22, 2024.
- The listing had an opening price of CHF 61.00 per share and implied a market capitalization around CHF 14.5 billion.
- The IPO placement volume was around CHF 2.0 billion, excluding the over-allotment option.
- SIX calls it the largest IPO placement in Switzerland since 2017.
- This source captures the capital-markets context for Galderma's public-company era.
- It is useful for understanding the ownership transition from private equity-backed asset to listed company.
- The release also helps explain why the annual report keeps referencing 2024 IPO momentum.
